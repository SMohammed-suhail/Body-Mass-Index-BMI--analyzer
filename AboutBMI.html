What is a BMI Calculator?

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. It provides a reliable indicator of body fatness for most people and is used to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]²

Where:

  • weight is in kilograms (kg)
  • height is in meters (m)

BMI Classification Table

Category BMI Range
Underweight Below 18.5
Normal weight 18.5 – 24.9
Overweight 25 – 29.9
Obese 30 and above

Why is BMI Important?

BMI gives an estimate of your body fat and related health risks. Although not perfect (since it doesn’t account for muscle mass, bone density, etc.), it is a helpful screening tool for weight-related health issues.

Limitations of BMI

  • Does not differentiate between fat and muscle mass
  • May not apply equally to athletes or elderly
  • Should be considered along with other health indicators
